About Me

As a Lubbock, Texas native with roots in the close-knit community of Post, I carry the values of hard work and determination that were instilled in me from a young age. My journey led me to the prestigious University of Oklahoma, where I pursued and completed a degree in Management Information Systems (MIS), a field that perfectly blends my interest in technology with business acumen. I enjoy spending time in the great outdoors, and I try never to say no to a road trip. Skiing, snowboarding, fishing, and college football are some of my passions. I am interested in most sports and sports statistics. In my spare time, I also enjoy gaming. I have a close-knit family and cherish moments with them and my friends.
